Associate Enterprise Architect

TransUnion Limited

What We'll Bring:

TransUnion HK Consumer Interactive requires a Lead Developer to assist Senior Enterprise Architect and Regional Head of IT to design enterprise architecture and lead the development team to deliver products in various projects. The Lead Developer also plays a role of Associate Enterprise Architect.

What You'll Bring:

  • Degree holder in Computer Science or related technology disciplines.
  • At least 10 years solid IT development experience including 3 years team leading experience.
  • Proficient in Java, Spring, Tomcat, Oracle DB and Linux.
  • Experience in developing mobile responsive web application and web service (both SOAP and REST).
  • Experience in coding with HTML, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Spring Boot, Spring MVC, Spring Security, Spring JPA, Hibernate, Thymeleaf, Linux shell script.
  • Experience in Agile methodologies and working with scrum team.
  • Experience in designing enterprise architecture is a big advantage.
  • Experience in developing native mobile app is an advantage.
  • Knowledge in Bitbucket, Jenkins, Artifactory, SonarQube, Veracode, Black Duck, IBM AppScan, Splunk, Robot Framework, SoapUI and F5 is an advantage.
  • Good verbal and written communication skills in English and Chinese is a must.

Impact You'll Make:

  • Deliver all assigned projects and tasks on time with satisfactory quality.
  • Assist Senior Enterprise Architect to design time-to-market enterprise architecture solution which covers business architecture, data architecture, application architecture and technology architecture.
  • Study US and other regions architecture solutions and reference them during architecture design.
  • Follow technology leaders’ directions and consider security findings by InfoSec and auditors during architecture design.
  • Provide proper development and testing effort estimation for any project.
  • Work with the scrum team and lead the developers to implement products.
  • Participate in the whole SDLC like gather requirements, design, implement, unit test, scan code, deploy, support and write documents.
  • Hands on coding is required.
  • Work with vendors, partners and clients for system integration.
  • Work with QA to deliver regression test automation.
  • Enhance developer’s code quality by conducting code review.
  • Improve developer’s security quality by running security scans.
  • Implement application performance, transaction and security monitoring with dashboard and alerts.
  • Leverage US technologies and tools to implement CI/CD for HK region.
  • Research technologies and present to different audience in different regions around the world.

Who We Are

A global leader in credit information and information management services, TransUnion gives businesses, consumers and the global community the power to achieve their goals. Businesses count on us to better manage risk and customer relationships. Consumers are able to better manage credit to achieve their financial goals. And in communities around the world we help build strong economies and give people the power to achieve their dreams.

Exceptional opportunities are coming as we build on this strong foundation. Our ambitious growth strategy includes substantial new investment worldwide, a wide range of new solutions to help our customers succeed like never before, and new ideas for expanding our reach in every part of our dynamic and fast-moving industry. We’re on an exciting journey and you can be a part of it.

 We are an equal opportunity employer and all qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability status, protected veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.

 Data collected will be treated in strict confidence and used for recruitment purposes only.

All applications applied through our system will be delivered directly to the advertiser and privacy of personal data of the applicant will be ensured with security.
